    • Heroes and class of heroes

    Humanoids

    The heroes of this race do not have to be human. These may be creatures with a human appearance, but their abilities and capabilities may indicate that they belong to another race.
    Among them there may be people who have risen from the dead, mutated or changed people, but they still remain humanoids.

    Demons

    Individuals of this race are distinguished by their terrifying appearance, they have burning eyes and frightening voices. It is difficult to identify the characteristic features of all representatives at once, but they all speak the language of demons - Ozkavosh.
    Some of them are creatures of hell, and each of them received their power through criminal means.

    Keen Folk

    The intelligent people are a race of humanoids, they are short in stature and resemble gnomes in appearance. They make up for their lack of physical strength with scientific inventions that make them stronger.

    Undead

    Not a race, but a state in which some heroes arrive. Living dead, victims of curses of defective immortality, or beings unable to permanently die.
    As usual, among them there are skeletons, ghosts and zombies. Some of the undead heroes were of a specific race in life, but nothing is known about others and their race remains in question.

    Satires

    Representatives of this race are distinguished by their horns and hooves, but not every representative has two characteristic features at once. They are also small in size and agile.
